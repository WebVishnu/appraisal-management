HR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM - SIMPLE FEATURES GUIDE
=============================================

WHAT IS THIS SYSTEM?
--------------------
This is a complete HR (Human Resources) management software that helps companies 
manage their employees, track attendance, handle leave requests, schedule shifts, 
conduct performance reviews, and monitor productivity.

WHO CAN USE IT?
--------------
There are 4 types of users:

1. SUPER ADMIN
   - Full control over the entire system
   - Can create and manage HR users and Managers
   - Can view all reports and data

2. HR (Human Resources)
   - Manages all employees
   - Creates and manages performance review cycles
   - Views and corrects attendance for everyone
   - Approves or rejects leave requests
   - Creates shifts and schedules
   - Reviews work reports
   - Views analytics and reports

3. MANAGER
   - Manages their team members
   - Reviews team performance
   - Views team attendance
   - Approves or rejects team leave requests
   - Reviews team work reports
   - Views team schedules and calendar
   - Sets team goals

4. EMPLOYEE
   - Views their own dashboard
   - Checks in and out for work
   - Applies for leave
   - Views their leave balance
   - Submits work reports
   - Fills self-assessment forms
   - Views their performance history
   - Views their assigned shifts


MAIN FEATURES
=============

1. EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT
   - Add new employees with unique ID numbers
   - Update employee information (name, email, role)
   - Assign managers to employees
   - Activate or deactivate employees
   - View employee profiles
   - Organize employees into teams

2. ATTENDANCE TRACKING
   - Employees check in when they arrive at work
   - Employees check out when they leave
   - System automatically detects if someone is late
   - System calculates working hours
   - HR can manually correct attendance records
   - View attendance history for any date range
   - Track attendance status: Present, Absent, Half Day, Missed Checkout

3. LEAVE MANAGEMENT
   - Employees can apply for leave (time off)
   - 5 types of leave:
     * Paid Leave
     * Unpaid Leave
     * Sick Leave
     * Casual Leave
     * Annual Leave
   - Managers approve or reject leave requests
   - System tracks leave balance for each employee
   - Leave balance shows: Total days, Used days, Available days
   - HR can manually adjust leave balances
   - System automatically deducts leave when approved
   - Employees can cancel pending leave requests

4. SHIFT & SCHEDULE MANAGEMENT
   - HR creates different work shifts (Morning, Evening, Night, etc.)
   - Each shift has:
     * Start time and end time
     * Break duration
     * Working days (Monday to Sunday)
   - HR assigns shifts to employees or teams
   - Shifts can be permanent or temporary
   - HR creates daily schedules (roster) for employees
   - Employees can request to swap shifts with each other
   - Managers approve shift swap requests
   - Employees can view their assigned shifts

5. PERFORMANCE APPRAISAL SYSTEM
   - HR creates performance review cycles
   - Each cycle has:
     * Start date and end date
     * Custom questions/competencies
   - Review process has 4 stages:
     * Draft - HR prepares the cycle
     * Open for Self Review - Employees fill their self-assessment
     * Open for Manager Review - Managers review their team
     * Closed - Reviews are finalized
   - Employees rate themselves on different skills
   - Managers provide feedback and ratings
   - System calculates final performance scores
   - Employees can view their performance history

6. WORK REPORTS
   - Employees submit daily or weekly work reports
   - Each report includes:
     * List of tasks completed
     * Time spent on each task
     * Key achievements
     * Challenges faced
     * Support needed
     * Self-rating of productivity
   - Tasks can be marked as: Completed, In Progress, or Blocked
   - Managers review and approve reports
   - Managers can provide feedback and ratings
   - Managers can flag reports (Excellent, Needs Support, Overloaded, etc.)
   - System calculates productivity scores
   - HR can view productivity analytics for the whole company

7. DASHBOARDS
   - Each user type has their own dashboard
   - Shows important information at a glance:
     * Statistics and numbers
     * Pending tasks
     * Recent activity
     * Charts and graphs
   - Quick access to common actions

8. NOTIFICATIONS
   - System sends notifications for important events:
     * When appraisal cycle starts
     * When review deadline is approaching
     * When leave is approved/rejected
     * When work report needs review
     * General reminders
   - Users see notification count on their dashboard
   - Users can mark notifications as read

9. REPORTS & ANALYTICS
   - HR can generate various reports:
     * Employee reports
     * Attendance reports
     * Leave reports
     * Performance reports
     * Productivity analytics
   - Reports can be filtered by:
     * Date range
     * Employee
     * Department
     * Status
   - Reports show charts and statistics
   - Can identify high performers
   - Can identify employees who need support


HOW IT WORKS
============

LOGIN
-----
- Users login with email and password
- System remembers who you are and what you can do
- Each user only sees what they're allowed to see

EMPLOYEE WORKFLOW
-----------------
1. Employee logs in
2. Checks in when arriving at work
3. Works throughout the day
4. Submits work report at end of day/week
5. Applies for leave when needed
6. Fills self-assessment during performance reviews
7. Views their attendance, leave balance, and performance

MANAGER WORKFLOW
----------------
1. Manager logs in
2. Views team dashboard
3. Reviews and approves leave requests from team
4. Reviews and approves work reports from team
5. Fills performance reviews for team members
6. Views team calendar and schedules
7. Monitors team productivity

HR WORKFLOW
-----------
1. HR logs in
2. Manages employee records
3. Creates performance review cycles
4. Views and corrects attendance records
5. Manages leave balances
6. Creates shifts and schedules
7. Reviews work reports
8. Generates reports and analytics
9. Views company-wide statistics

SUPER ADMIN WORKFLOW
--------------------
1. Super Admin logs in
2. Creates and manages HR users and Managers
3. Views all system reports
4. Has access to everything
